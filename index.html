Discovery Questions for Prospecting
  • When was your last comprehensive HIPAA risk assessment? By internal team or external consultant?
  • How are you currently managing and tracking remediation of identified vulnerabilities?
  • Do you have documented policies covering all 18 HIPAA Security Rule categories?
  • How do you manage Business Associate Agreements? Do you have a BAA tracking system?
  • Have you had any OCR inquiries, audits, or breach notifications in the last 3 years?
  • Are you responsible for HIPAA compliance across multiple locations or entities?
  • What's your current training approach? How often and how do you track completion?
  • Do you use any compliance/GRC platform today? What are the gaps?

Buyer Personas

The Overwhelmed Compliance Officer

Primary Champion

Wears 3+ hats. Knows they need a better SRA but lacks time and tools. Fears personal liability. Wants someone to tell them exactly what to do.

The Protective IT Director

Technical Evaluator

Cares about actual security, not compliance theater. Wants NVA + SRA together. Needs to present risk posture to leadership.

The Risk-Averse CEO

Decision Maker

Reads about breaches in the news. Doesn't want to be next. Needs simple ROI explanation. "Are we protected?"

The Small Practice Owner

SPSRA Target · Tier 1B

Dentist, therapist, or physician running 1-20 person practice. Budget-conscious. SPSRA at $499/yr is their entry point.

The Office Manager

SPSRA User · Tier 1B

Does everything. Handles billing, HR, IT, compliance. Needs something simple that doesn't require a compliance background.

The New Leader

Change Agent

Recently hired. Wants to professionalize operations. Has budget authority. Our best inbound signal.
